ABOUT US

Why Us


Here at Hana's Garden, we promise to deliver you the perfect flower for any occation. Just simply tell us your preferences and we will make your blooming dreams come to life.

Our Mission


Flowers are more than just pretty things. They are memories, emotions and expressions of love. Hana's Garden aims to create beautiful moments for our customers with luxury flowers.

Our Promise


We deliver optimistic and diverse flowers to our customers. We take the time and care to create each floral design for you because we take pride in the work we do and want the recipient to be happy with what we have made.

Our Story


It started with a love story and a love for flowers. This blossomed into a flourishing business that is Hana's Garden. Together, our founders sought a way to disrupt the florist industry by introducing the elements of e-commerce and a personalized delivery service. For many years, we are based in the Georgetown, Penang but our orders are placed from people across the world to be delivered to their loved ones in Malaysia. Our integrity is supported by the “Hana's Garden Guarantee” which guarantees the unique freshness, superior flower quality and competitive value of every single order from our garden.
